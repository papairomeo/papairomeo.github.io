Letter 3 · Feeling

Our gaming sessions

June 22, 2026

Son,

Today is June 22, 2026. Today I remembered our gaming sessions on Roblox, and it made me miss those days. It's been a while since we played together.

I was never good at it, and you always knew that. But whenever we connected, you were excited to show me everything, to explain the characters, the powers of each one. You always wanted to be the best, and you were. And I was there trying to keep up, without much of a chance.

There is something very you in those moments. A seriousness when explaining things, an excitement when things go right. Playing with you, I learn a lot about who you are.

Sometimes you are playing with Angus and I stay nearby, watching. The two of you disagree about things in the game, go on missions together, help each other out. I can see that you like to lead, to drive things forward, and that you have been learning to do that alongside your friends.

I always have so much fun in our gaming sessions together, even losing every time. I miss that. Whenever you want to play, I am here.

With love,
Papai
