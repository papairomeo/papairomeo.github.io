Letter 4 · Feeling

Guts & Blackpowder

July 6, 2026

Son,

Today is July 6, 2026. I've been thinking about our last call, which was on June 26th. You called me, and when the phone rang and it was you, I had a smile on my face that took a long time to go away.

You told me about a cultural event at school, with food from all over the world. I was curious to know what you tried and what you found strange enough to not even go near.

You told me you switched subjects, from DigiTech to FoodTech this semester. When I asked, you said you wanted to try something different. I kept thinking about that. I've always wanted to show you more about what I do, about technology, how that world works from the inside. But the distance and the little time we have together haven't been enough for that. That makes me a little sad. Not because you chose FoodTech, but because I feel I haven't been present enough to be a reference in that choice, the way I would have liked to be.

And then we played Guts & Blackpowder. In that game, you pick an army and fight together against hordes of zombies, moving through cities and mazes full of surprises. Both of us on the same side, helping each other, saving each other. It was good to fight alongside you like that.

With love,
Papai
