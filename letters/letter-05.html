Letter 5 · Feeling

We have an agreement

July 12, 2026

Son,

Today is July 12, 2026. I've been thinking about our call last Friday, July 10th.

You asked me not to text your mom when I want to talk to you. To text you directly instead. I heard that, and I was happy, but I also had to be honest. I explained that I had tried many times before, that I would send you messages and you wouldn't answer. That the only way I found to reach you was by asking your mom to let you know. That I don't like doing that, that I prefer talking directly to you, just you. But when you ignore me, I have no other choice.

You understood. You told me about the do not disturb mode on your phone, that my messages were ending up at the bottom where you couldn't see them. I understood too.

And then we made an agreement. I'll try to reach you directly. If you don't answer, I'll try again. And one more time. Only after that will I contact your mom. You thought that was fair.

I thought it was more than fair. I felt happy, relieved. I came out of that conversation feeling like we had worked something important out in a mature way, no fighting, no hard feelings. Just the two of us talking it through.

Afterwards we had fun the way we usually do, playing together on Roblox. But what stayed with me was the agreement. It was good, son.

With love,
Papai
